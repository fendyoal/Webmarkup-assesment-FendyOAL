Solar energy is the way to go

Solar energy is radiant light and heat from the Sun that is harnessed using a range of evolving technologies such as solar heating, photovoltaics, solar thermal energy, molten salt power plants and artificial photosynthesis. We believe that solar energy should be utilised everywhere as it has a lot of benefits including the reduction of global warming.
